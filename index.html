The Problem

Many children struggle with times tables.

Traditional memorisation methods can leave children feeling frustrated, lacking confidence and disengaged from maths.

With the Year 4 Multiplication Tables Check (MTC) now part of the national curriculum, schools are under increasing pressure to help every child succeed.

Tunes Times Tables offers a different approach.
The Solution

Movement, Rhymes & Fun

Tunes Times Tables teaches multiplication through rhythm, movement and participation, helping children remember answers faster while building confidence.

Children are never placed in front of screens until they know their times tables and their confidence is built.

Meet the Creator

About Mr NK

Clifford Harding, known as Mr NK, created Tunes Times Tables to help children master multiplication through Movement, Rhymes & Fun.

For over 20 years, he has worked with schools across the UK, helping thousands of children build confidence and succeed in mathematics.

His mission is to awaken the inner genius in every child.
